Refund Policy

Effective Date: June 17, 2026

At Trip2Movie, our goal is to help you relive your best memories perfectly. We stand behind the quality of our cinematic films, which is why we offer a straightforward, no-nonsense refund policy.

1. Love It or It's Free Guarantee

If you purchase a Standard or Cinema 4K film and you are not completely satisfied with the final result, you are eligible for a full 100% refund. No hidden fees, no complex forms.

2. Timeframe for Refunds

To qualify for a refund, you must submit your request within 14 days of receiving your final rendered film. After 14 days, we consider the transaction final as the computational rendering costs have already been fully incurred on our end.

3. How to Request a Refund

Requesting a refund is simple:

  • Email us at [Insert Contact Email] from the email address associated with your Trip2Movie account.
  • Include your order number or the link to the film you are unhappy with.
  • Tell us briefly why you weren't satisfied (this is optional, but helps us improve!).

4. Processing Your Refund

Once we receive your email, we will process your refund within 2-3 business days. The funds will automatically be applied to your original method of payment. Please note that it may take an additional 5-10 business days for your bank or credit card company to officially post the refund to your account.

5. Gift Purchases

If you purchased a "Gift a film" package, the refund must be requested by the original purchaser (the person who paid). Once a gift link has been successfully redeemed and rendered by the recipient, the same 14-day "Love It or It's Free" guarantee applies, but the refund will be routed back to the original purchaser's payment method.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding our refund policy, please don't hesitate to reach out to us at [Insert Contact Email].
