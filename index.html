Good to know

Questions, answered.

Where do my photos go? Are they private? +

Your memories are yours. Uploads are private to your account, never sold, and never used to train anything. You can delete them — and your films — whenever you want.

Does the film have sound and music? +

Yes — it’s the heart of it. Your video clips keep their original audio, so the laughter and the waves come back, and we lay an original score underneath. You can swap the music to fit the mood.

What do I need to upload? +

Whatever you have. Phone photos and short clips are ideal. There’s no minimum — though the more moments you add, the richer the film. We handle the ordering, pacing and music.

How long is the finished film? +

Most films land around one to three minutes — short enough to actually watch, long enough to feel like the trip. Paid plans unlock longer cuts.

When can I actually use it? +

We’re rolling out invites from the private beta in small groups each week. Join the list and we’ll email you the moment your spot opens.
